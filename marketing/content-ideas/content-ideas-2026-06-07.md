## 1. The Bath Time Argument
- **Content type:** Monday — Founder story
- **Hook line:** "In 2001, I made my daughter cry at bath time. Here's what happened next."
- **Format:** Reel
- **Visual direction:** Dad sits on the edge of an *empty* bathtub (fully clothed, casual evening clothes), holding a worn notebook. Cut to close-ups of his hand writing at a kitchen table late at night — lamp on, everyone else asleep. End on the notebook's first page, dated 2001.
- **Caption draft:** "After a bath time argument that ended in yelling and tears, my wife said: You need to think about how you want the girls to remember you. I started writing that night. 25 years later, it's an app. I'm not a child psychologist. I'm just a dad who didn't want one bad night to be the night they remembered."
- **Pack focus:** Bath 🛁 (origin story), Brand / All packs
