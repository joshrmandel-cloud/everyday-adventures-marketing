## 1. The Notebook Still Works
- **Content type:** Monday — Founder story
- **Hook line:** "My daughters are 30 and 26 now. The notebook still works."
- **Format:** Reel
- **Visual direction:** Dad's hands (fully clothed, casual home clothes) flipping through an old spiral notebook at a kitchen table — handwriting from 2001, dog-eared pages, a coffee ring on the cover. Cut to the same hands holding a phone, opening the Everyday Adventures app, scrolling to an activity that matches a page in the notebook.
- **Caption draft:** "Twenty-five years ago I wrote these down for two little girls who are now grown adults with their own lives. I tested one on a friend's toddler last month, just to be sure. Same activity, same age, same result — works exactly the same."
- **Pack focus:** Brand / All packs
